Your Meals, Your Way!

Shopping and meal planning can be a weekly (or daily!) struggle, but it doesn't have to be. That's why we made mealtime, a central location for all your recipes and tasty meal plans. The best part is, when you're ready to head to the store, you can turn your plan into a shopping list in a single click - no more forgetting crucial ingredients!

Enter Your Recipes

Whether it's a delicious personal recipe or something you saw online, add your recipes to our virtual recipe book. Keep track of ingredients, cooking instructions and all other aspects of your favorite foods!

Plan Your Meals

Make your week easier by adding your favorite recipes to your personal menu and plan as many days ahead as you want. Easily see your meals on the go to keep track of what's coming up!

Create Shopping List

With the data from your recipes and meal plan, we can generate an interactive shopping list to simplify your shopping experience and ensure you have all the ingredients you need!

Meal Prep Your Way

Whether you're a meal-prep fanatic, a family needing some organization, or just someone who doesn't want to have to keep running out to the grocery store, this app can help! What are you waiting for, take a load off and try it out!
